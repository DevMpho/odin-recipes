Prawn Pasta

Description

Garlic prawn pasta is a savory dish that brings together the rich flavors of seafood and the boldness of garlic

  • 250g pasta (linguine, spaghetti, or fettuccine)
  • 300g prawns, peeled and deveined
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta in a large pot of salted boiling water according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside, reserving a cup of pasta cooking water.
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add minced garlic and saut√© for about a minute until fragrant, being careful not to burn it.
  3. Add the prawns to the skillet and cook for 2-3 minutes until they turn pink and opaque. Season with salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes if using.
  4. Add the halved cherry tomatoes to the skillet and cook for another 2-3 minutes until they start to soften.
  5. Add the cooked pasta to the skillet along with a splash of pasta cooking water. Toss everything together to coat the pasta with the garlic prawn sauce. Adjust the seasoning if needed.
  6. Remove the skillet from heat and sprinkle chopped parsley over the pasta. Optionally, sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese on top for extra flavor.
  7. Serve the garlic prawn pasta hot, garnished with more fresh parsley and Parmesan cheese if desired.
