Pancakes

Description

This quick American pancake recipe yields a light and fluffy pancakes ideal for a weekend brunch or a quick breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/2 tsp Baking Powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • melted butter

Steps

  1. Mix dry ingredients together.
  2. Add wet ingredients to dry ingredients.
  3. Whisk well until you have a creamy thick batter.
  4. Add tbsp of oil to pan on a medium high heat.
  5. Add about 1/4 of pancake batter to oil(for one pancake).
  6. Once the edges are rounded and bubbles start to show up on pancake,carefully use spatula to flip the pancakes over.
  7. Let the pancake cook for a min and half more then remove.
